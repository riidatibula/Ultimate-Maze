Help

Controls:
Arrow up: move player up
Arrow down: move player down
Arrow right: move player right
Arrow left: move player left

Gameplay:
Reach the home located at the center of the Maze within a minute.
Collect blue dots to add time.
But colliding with red dots will deduct time.
Contains portals which will transport you instantly.
The Maze will change for every 30 seconds.
Your final score is the remaining time after reaching the home.
Good luck! :D
